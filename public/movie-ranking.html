The Best Movies According to Jelena

My top 5 movies of all time.

Portrait of a Lady on Fire

This is my favourite movie of all time. Everything in it speaks to my soul.

Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

This is my favourite anime of all time. And Nausicaä is one of my favourite characters of all time.

What a Feeling

This is my current favourite movie. Lesbian rom coms are my catnip.

Aliens

This is my favourite horror movie of all time. Ripley is everything to me.

Wild Robot

This is my favourite cartoon i watched in forever. Made me cry the whole way through.
Lupita Nyong'o did absolutely fantastic job voicing Roz, her warm voice is balm for the soul.
